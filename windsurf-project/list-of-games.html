PC-98 Era

Highly Responsive to Prayers

1996

The game that started it all! Despite what the rest of the series would become, Touhou 1 is a breakout styled game.

Story of Eastern Wonderland

1997

The very first game in the series to feature the danmaku gameplay Touhou is known for!

Phantasmagoria of Dim. Dream

1997

A versus-style danmaku game, the first in the series!

Lotus Land Story

1998

A return to the Danmaku gameplay.

Mystic Square

1998

The final PC-98 game. It is the most refined of the PC-98 games.

Early Windows Era

The Embodiment of Scarlet Devil

2002

The first Windows game in the series, introducing the iconic danmaku gameplay.

Perfect Cherry Blossom

2003

Introduces the Cherry Point system and features the four seasons theme.

Imperishable Night

2004

Features a unique partner system and time-based mechanics.

Phantasmagoria of Flower View

2005

A versus-style danmaku entry, spiritual successor to PoDD.

Middle Windows Era

Mountain of Faith

2007

A sort of reboot for the series. Almost completely overhauling Touhou. It's influence is still seen in Touhou games today!

Subterranean Animism

2008

Features a similar partner system as Imperishable Night.

Undefined Fantastic Object

2009

Added collectable UFOs. Similar mechanics would be introduced in later games.

Ten Desires

2011

Introduced the Spirit Point and Trance mechanics.

Double Dealing Character

2013

A return to simpler gameplay, with varied shot types.

Current Windows Era

Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom

2015

First and only game to use Point Device mode due to its intense difficulty. You will need it...

Hidden Star in Four Seasons

2017

Introduced a seasons mechanic.

Wily Beast and Weakest Creature

2019

A re-introduction of UFO's main mechanic, now with animal spirits instead of UFOs!

Unconnected Marketeers

2021

Introduced a card power-up system. Providing a unique mix of danmaku and money management!

Unfinished Dream of All Living Ghost

2023

The first game since Touhou 9 to bring back the versus multiplayer gameplay.

Fossilized Wonders

2025

The latest Touhou game with a focus on current world events to tell its story.

SPIN-OFF GAMES

Touhou has numerous spin-offs of many different genres, often providing more insight into characters and their stories!

Photography Games

Shoot the Bullet

2005

The first photography danmaku game, featuring Aya as the main character!

Double Spoiler

2010

Sequel to Shoot the Bullet, featuring Aya and Hatate Himekaidou as playable characters.

Violet Detector

2018

Another photography danmaku game with Sumireko as the lead this time.

Danmaku Spin-Offs

Great Fairy Wars

2010

Features Cirno as the main character with an ice-based spell card system.

Impossible Spell Card

2014

Seija takes the lead this time. Features difficult spell cards, but powerful items to help you survive!

100th Black Market

2022

Similar to Unconnected Marketeers, with the fan-favorite Marisa Kirisame as the lead.

Twilight Frontier Games (Fighting Games)

Immaterial and Missing Power

2004

The first fighting game, setting the stage for the rest of the games.

Scarlet Weather Rhapsody

2008

Similar to Immaterial and Missing Power, now with weather effects that can change the tide of the battle!

Hisoutensoku

2009

It's enhanced Scarlet Weather Rhapsody.

Hopeless Masquerade

2013

An almost complete rework of the gameplay, now with a focus on aerial combat!

Urban Legend in Limbo

2015

Aerial combat.

Antinomy of Common Flowers

2017

A rework of the gameplay, with a master and slave, yes this is what it is called, system, similar to the partner system seen in the danmaku games.

Sunken Fossil World

2021

Quite literally described as a "horizontal side-scrolling danmaku water action game"
