IMPORTANT TERMS

Specific terminology for Touhou games.

← Back to Where to Start

Danmaku

Literally means "bullet curtain" Danmaku can refer to a genre of game, and the literal bullets an enemy fires at you.

Hitbox

The small area of your character that when touched by a bullet or enemy, will register a hit or death. In most Touhou games, this is just a tiny dot at the center of your character. This term is not specific to Touhou.

Bomb

A powerful attack that clears bullets on screen and deals damage to enemies. Usually limited in number.

Spell Card

Special attack patterns used by bosses, often with unique names and patterns.

Non-Spell

Also called Nons, these are patterns which are not spell cards, and are usually between spell cards.

Graze

When a bullet comes very close to your hitbox without hitting it. Some games reward grazing with score or resources, such as Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom.
